Book Restorations

We specialise in the repair and restoration, conservation and preservation of damaged or old books in need of attention. Your book will be assessed prior to any work being started to determine the best approach. Depending on the extent of the damage and the desired result, the restoration can be as simple or extensive as required. In some cases, the best option to preserve the value of a very rare or heavily damaged book is to create an archival storage box enabling the book to be stored safely in it's current state and avoiding further damage.

Please feel free to contact us via Enquiry page to arrange an inspection for determining the best option for your book.

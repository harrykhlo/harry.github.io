Ye Olde Home

The Ye Olde Book Shop became an instant mecca for a book-hungry Carnegie Hill when it opened in 2001. Located in a landmark neighborhood in Christchurch City, we have been satisfying the literary desires of this residential neighborhood about two decades. We take enormous pride in our stock, and carefully select current fiction and nonfiction titles to bring only the best of the best to our customers. Our commitment to carrying “only the best of the best” extends to all sections of the store including history, biography, travel, cook books, parenting, poetry, mysteries, and art.

We traveled around the world to attend book auctions. On the way home, we would like to hunt for characteristic book-related ornaments, collecting those precious bookstands from various parts of the world. Check it out when you are walking pass. Have fun!

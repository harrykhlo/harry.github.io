About Us

Whether you are interested in children’s books or adult books, our dedicated and knowledgeable staff of bookstore professionals is on hand to answer your questions or offer suggestions as needed. We can order any current title that is not in stock and have it in a day or two, and we also search for elusive out-of-print titles. Inquiries and orders can also be handled by phone without the frustration of automated systems and menus. Gift wrapping is complimentary and telephone, email, and internet orders are shipped immediately. In person or by phone, our service is always personal. Many out-of-town customers, who discovered us when visiting Carnegie Hill, have become long-distance regulars, preferring our personal service to the anonymity of the Internet and secure in the knowledge that their requests will receive the closest possible attention.
